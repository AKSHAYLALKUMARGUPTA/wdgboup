PRINCIPAL MESSAGE

Let’s work together to take our school to the next level of excellence.

We are a school with a difference. We value nationalism, creativity, innovation, dedication, honesty, and strive to nurture them in our students. Shaping young, susceptible minds is one of life's biggest challenges. At SVM Kamla Nagar Agra, we aim to empower our students to grow as individuals with ethics and strong open discerning minds with an international perspective, preparing them to make a mark in the global village, the world has come to be today. Our main facilities are excellent infrastructure, Laboratory, Knowledge Hub, IT - Multimedia Lab, encouraging environment and dedicated faculty which comprises of highly qualified and experienced teachers.

We are one of those few schools in Agra that practice a system of inclusive education. For that Vidya Bharati made it the very basis of her system of education baptized as 'Saraswati Panchapadiya Shiksha Vidhi' . These five steps are : 1. Adhiti, 2. Bodh, 3. Abhiyas, 4. Prayog, 5. Prasar-swadhyay and Pravachan and the philosophy of all round development of child is based on unfoldmans of five koshas i.e. Annamaya Kosha, Pranmaya Kosha, Manomaya Kosha, Jyannamaya Kosha & Ananamaya Kosha as envisaged in our upanishidie philosophy. Our highly qualified team of Special Educators ensure that children with diverse needs are given individual attention. We encourage personal expression and celebrate experimentation by every learner. Besides doing well academically, our kids are involved in the fields of sports and culture and engaging in community service.
